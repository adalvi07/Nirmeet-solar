About us

Nirmeet is an end to end solutions provider for solar energy, based in Mumbai. We design high performing and cost-effective solar solutions customized to your electricity needs, system preferences and budget that will maximize your financial returns. We help you build savings through clean energy.

Our expertise is designing and engineering solar installations after in-depth energy analysis, shadow analysis using software simulations and integrating latest solar technologies in our solutions. We are a team of qualified engineers with long experience in reputed multinationals. The processes we follow in procurement and execution are elaborate and professional.

Our Team
